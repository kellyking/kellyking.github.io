Holiday Customer Journey Analysis

Overview

During our Holiday season (Thanksgiving to Christmas) I regularly analyze different sets of metrics to determine the overall effectiveness of our marketing campaign. One key area is our customers journey through our website.

Skills

Journey Analysis
Wireframing

Tools

Google Analytics

Problem

A few weeks into our holiday season, I noticed that a large portion of our customers who were on our Gift Guide were navigating to our Gift Guide (looping).

After looking at click rates and different user segments, I found that 26.1% of people who were on the Gift Guide page were clicking through the main navigation to go to the Gift Gudie page.

Research

We hypothesized that the main reason customers were navigating to the same page that they were on, was the vast similarities between the homepage and the Gift Guide (above the fold). When customers were landing on our Gift Guide, they perhaps thought they were on our homepage and tried to go to the Gift Guide.

Solution

Working with the Digital Designers, we come up with a solution with a quick turn-a-round. We did a quick competitive analysis of Gift Guides on other ecom sites, and chose to incorporate helpful category-type collections. The above-the-fold portion of the Gift Guide was redesigned to looked vastly different from the homepage.

Results

After deploying our new Gift Guide design, the looping behavior dropped significantly, to about 6% of users.
