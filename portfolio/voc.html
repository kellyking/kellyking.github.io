Voice of Customer Program Development (Ecommerce)

  • Role: Lead UX Researcher
  • Scope: Customer feedback strategy, platform integration, and journey-wide insight collection
  • Methods: Vendor evaluation, stakeholder interviews, program design, survey design
  • Impact: Established a scalable VOC framework to continuously inform customer experience improvements

Context

To better understand our customers' experiences across the ecommerce journey, I led the development and implementation of a Voice of Customer (VOC) program. The goal was to gather actionable feedback at key touchpoints, ensure insights could inform product and marketing decisions, and create a repeatable framework for ongoing improvement.

This effort required aligning multiple stakeholders, integrating a new vendor into our tech stack, and designing a sustainable feedback strategy.

Problem Identification

While our site collected some customer behavior data, we lacked structured mechanisms to capture qualitative feedback directly from users at critical points in the shopping journey. This limited our ability to:

  • Identify friction points and delight moments
  • Prioritize enhancements based on customer needs
  • Measure experience improvements over time

Research & Vendor Evaluation

I conducted a structured research and evaluation process to select a VOC platform:

  • Compiled a list of four potential vendors from colleague recommendations, analytics partners, and top search results
  • Conducted vendor interviews and reviewed platform capabilities against functional requirements
  • Developed two proof-of-concepts (POCs) and beta-tested them internally
  • Evaluated each vendor on scalability, flexibility, and ability to integrate with our ecommerce tech stack

Concurrently, I researched VOC best practices and developed a comprehensive proposal for leadership, including:

  • Goals for 6-month and 1-year feedback initiatives
  • Survey design and deployment plan
  • Framework for gathering input from cross-functional teams (Product, Marketing, CX)

The proposal, including a concise slide deck, received full leadership approval for implementation.

Solution & Implementation

We implemented the chosen VOC platform and launched initial tests in a QA environment:

  • Persistent feedback button: Collects general customer experience feedback across the site
  • Targeted pop-up survey: Focused on bridal customers selecting their ring setting, capturing context-specific insights

The framework allows us to continuously iterate on survey design, collect actionable feedback, and feed insights back to Product and UX teams for experience improvements.

Results & Impact

  • Successfully integrated a scalable VOC platform into the ecommerce tech stack
  • Established a repeatable, cross-functional feedback process for ongoing customer experience improvements
  • Laid the groundwork for data-driven prioritization of site enhancements and roadmap decisions

This is an ongoing program; future iterations will leverage VOC data to inform journey optimizations, UX improvements, and product strategy.

Lead Researcher Contribution

  • Evaluated vendors and led proof-of-concept testing
  • Aligned leadership and cross-functional teams around a scalable feedback framework
  • Designed surveys and feedback collection mechanisms grounded in UX research best practices
  • Established an iterative process for continuous improvement across the customer journey
