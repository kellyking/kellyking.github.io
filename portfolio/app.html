Mobile App Redesign & UX Optimization

  • Role: Lead UX Researcher
  • Scope: End-to-end mobile app experience (iOS & Android)
  • Methods: Qualitative analysis, paper prototypes, interactive prototype testing, accessibility review
  • Impact: Improved app usability and feature adoption; maintained 6% purchase conversion

Context

Our mobile app allows customers to access media they purchasedâ€”ebooks, videos, and audio files. Over time, customer feedback indicated dissatisfaction due to missing features commonly expected in similar apps (iTunes, Netflix, Audible), coupled with infrequent updates.

I was tasked with analyzing customer feedback, usage patterns, and accessibility considerations to guide a redesign that improved usability, incorporated high-priority customer requests, and ensured an inclusive experience.

Problem Identification

Analysis of hundreds of app store reviews (iOS & Android) revealed key challenges:

  • Initial excitement about the app faded due to missing features and lack of updates
  • Customers expected integrated media consumption (read, watch, listen) within the app
  • Certain design and interaction elements caused minor usability friction, potentially impacting task completion and conversion

Objective: Prioritize improvements that enhance core media consumption flows, address high-impact customer requests, and maintain accessibility standards.

Research & Insights

I led a mixed-methods investigation to uncover pain points and opportunities:

  • Qualitative coding of app reviews quantified major themes and top customer requests
  • Low-fidelity paper prototype tests with end users highlighted usability tweaks (e.g., page headings, interaction clarity)
  • Remote interactive prototype testing validated high-fidelity designs with realistic tasks and flows
  • Accessibility review (WCAG 2.1 AA) ensured inclusive navigation and media access, including VoiceOver testing

Insights indicated that consolidating reading, watching, and listening experiences in a single, seamless interface would address the majority of customer frustration and increase engagement.

Design & Iteration

Working closely with Digital Design and Product teams:

  • Translated research findings into low- and high-fidelity wireframes and prototypes
  • Incorporated top feature requests within MVP constraints
  • Iteratively refined interaction flows through rapid testing rounds
  • Conducted a beta test with 100+ internal users, capturing end-to-end task completion and survey feedback

Feedback from these iterations guided minor adjustments, ensuring a polished, user-centered experience at launch.

Results & Impact

  • Redesigned app launched successfully with all usability improvements from testing incorporated
  • Maintained 6% conversion rate for purchases, validating that the new flows supported transactional goals
  • Positive qualitative feedback from both beta and post-launch users
  • Accessibility compliance ensured a more inclusive experience for all users

Lead Researcher Contribution

  • Synthesized hundreds of qualitative reviews into actionable insights
  • Designed and executed multi-stage usability testing (paper, interactive, beta)
  • Influenced design decisions through evidence-based recommendations while balancing MVP constraints
  • Conducted accessibility review to ensure WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
  • Measured outcomes in both behavioral and business metrics
